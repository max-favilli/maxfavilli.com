This is the first post.

I'm starting this blog to write down the things I think about long enough that they're worth writing down — mostly around IT, AI, investing, politics, movies, and whatever else refuses to stay inside one category.

## What this is

- **A thinking outlet.** I write to think. If a post changes my mind, it worked.
- **Cross-posted.** Longer pieces will also appear on Substack, and the comments live there.
- **Slow.** I'd rather publish less and mean more.

## What this isn't

- A newsletter-as-a-product. I'm not optimizing for subscriber counts.
- A hot-take feed. If I can't hold an opinion for a week, it's not worth publishing.
- A portfolio. The code, the day job, and the essays are separate things on purpose.

## Where to start

As of today there are two real posts to read:

- [The Future of AI — part 1](/posts/the-future-of-ai-part-1/) on how AI is reshaping the make-or-buy and software-vs-hardware lines.
- [12 Mistakes Every Investor Makes According to Warren Buffett](/posts/12-mistakes-every-investor-makes-according-to-warren-buffett/) — a working list.

More to come. If any of this is useful to you, that's the whole point.
