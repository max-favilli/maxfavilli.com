One could write an encyclopedia with the good reasons why it's stupid to start-up a company in Italy.

The other day I was looking for a solution to manage outbound and inbound text messages aka SMS, there are plenty of good providers on the web, with nice simple API and a reasonable cost: [nexmo.com](https://www.nexmo.com/), [plivo.com](http://plivo.com/), [twilio.com](https://www.twilio.com/)... They operate in many countries: USA, UK, Germany, etc... Including places like Spain, Finland, Pakistan... I mean "Pakistan"... But not in Italy... Italy is really the third world for business, and the third world for IT... Christ...
